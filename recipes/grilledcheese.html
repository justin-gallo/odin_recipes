Grilled Cheese

Description:

This is the grilled cheese recipe my parents made for me as a kid.

Ingredients:

  • White Bread
  • Butter
  • American Cheese (two slices)
  • Ketchup

Steps:

  1. Butter one side of each of two slices of bread.
  2. Turn on a pan/griddle to medium low heat.
  3. As the pan begins heating, place one slice of bread in the pan, butter side down.
  4. Add two slices of cheese to the bread in the pan, and top with the second slice of bread (butter side up).
  5. Wait approx. 5 minutes, then flip. Wait until bread is sufficiently toasted, then remove from heat.
  6. Cut in half, and serve with an excessively large portion of ketchup.
