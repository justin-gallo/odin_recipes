Margarita

This margarita recipe is sure to delight. Like a classic margarita, but spicy to give an extra little kick and pizzaz.

Ingredients:

  • Triple Sec
  • Tequilla
  • Tajin Chili-Lime Seasoning
  • Lime Juice
  • Simple Syrup

Steps:

  1. Add ice to shaker cup.
  2. Add 2oz Tequilla, 1oz Triple Sec, 0.75oz Lime Juice, and 1oz Simple Syrup to shaker glass.
  3. Shake until sufficiently mixed.
  4. Soak rim in Jalepeno Juice, and salt rim with Tajin.
  5. Strain cocktail over fresh ice.
  6. Garnish with lime wedge and serve.
